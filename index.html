WELCOME TO MGM's COLLEGE OF COMPUTER SCIENCE & IT,NANDED

Mahatma Gandhi Mission's College of Computer Science and IT is located in Nanded, a district in Maharashtra.
The college was established in 2000.
It is approved by the Government of Maharashtra and affiliated with Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded.
