Our Facilities

MGM's College of Computer Science & IT is committed to providing excellent
infrastructure and facilities to ensure a conducive learning environment for students.
Our campus is spread over 20 acres and is easily distinguished by its cleanliness, greenery, and beauty.

Classroom

  • Smart boards and digital presentation systems
  • Proper lighting and ventilation for focused learning
  • Audio-visual equipment for interactive sessions
  • ICT-enabled teaching infrastructure
  • Spacious rooms with adequate seating capacity

Computer Lab

  • Clean and organized lab setup
  • Comfortable seating for students
  • Systems arranged for smooth practical sessions
  • Guidance available during lab timings
  • Supports project work, research and technical learning.
  • High-speed internet access for students.

Scinence Lab

  • Well-equipped science laboratory with modern instruments.
  • Provides practical learning experience for students.
  • Safe and spacious environment for experiments.
  • Supports physics, chemistry and biology practical sessions.
  • Guided by experienced faculty and lab assistants.
